## ðŸ”§ TROUBLESHOOTING

### "My site isn't showing in Google yet"
- Wait 2-4 weeks for initial indexing
- Make sure you submitted sitemap.xml to Search Console
- Check robots.txt allows crawling
- Verify website is accessible and has no crawl errors

### "Traffic is low"
- Ensure Google My Business is optimized with photos
- Add more reviews (more social proof = higher CTR)
- Improve title tags and meta descriptions (affects CTR)
- Create more content targeting local searches

### "No phone calls from Google"
- Make sure phone number is prominent on website
- Ensure phone number is in Google My Business
- Add click-to-call buttons on mobile
- Make sure business hours are accurate
